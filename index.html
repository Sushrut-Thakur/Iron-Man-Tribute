Who is Iron Man

Anthony Edward "Tony" Stark was a billionaire industrialist, a founding member of the Avengers, and the former CEO of Stark Industries. A brash but brilliant inventor, Stark was self-described as a genius, billionaire, playboy, and philanthropist. With his great wealth and exceptional technical knowledge, Stark was one of the world's most powerful men following the deaths of his parents and enjoyed the playboy lifestyle for many years until he was kidnapped by the Ten Rings in Afghanistan, while demonstrating a fleet of Jericho missiles.
With his life on the line, Stark created an armored suit which he used to escape his captors. Upon returning home, he utilized several more armors to use against terrorists, as well as Obadiah Stane who turned against Stark. Stark enjoyed the fame that came with his new secret identity and decided to share it with the world, publicly revealing himself as Iron Man.

A time line of Tony Stark's life

  • 1970 - Born in Manhattan, New York
  • 1974 - At a very young age of 4 years, he designed his first circuit board
  • 1977 - At the age of 7, he built a V8 motorcycle engine
  • 1987 - Stark graduated summa cum laude from MIT
  • 1991 - Inherited Stark Industries a few months after the death of his parents
  • 2008 - Kidnapped by the terrorist organisation "The Ten Rings", created a miniature model of the arc reactor and the Iron Man armour mk.1. The first "I am Iron Man".
  • 2010 - Discovered a new element (Vibranium) and synthesised it to substitute Palladium in the arc reactor.
  • 2012 - Saved the world by carrying a nuke fired at Manhattan through a wormhole onto the Chitauri base.
  • 2013 - Had a surgery to get the shrapnel out of his chest so that he won't have to depend on the arc reactor to keep him alive.
  • 2015 - Defeated Ultron and saved the world from global extinction.
  • 2016 - Stark signed the Sokovia accords, which led to the Civil War which tore the Avengers apart.
  • 2017 - A mere mortal makes a Titan bleed. Thanos knew and respected Stark, but never expected him to put up such a fight. That too, while the mad Titan was in possession of 4 infinity stones.
  • 2023 - Invents a time-space GPS. Defeats Thanos and his army. The final "I am Iron Man"
